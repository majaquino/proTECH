What is Malware?

Malware is software that is installed on a computer without the user's consent and that performs malicious actions, such as stealing passwords or money. There are many ways to detect malware, but the most common is to scan the computer for malicious files or programs.

Read More

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cybercrime in which a target or targets are contacted by email, telephone or text message by someone posing as a legitimate institution to lure individuals into providing sensitive data such as personally identifiable information, banking and credit card details, and passwords.

Read More

Protect Yourself and Your Devices

Today, our lives are intertwined with internet-connected devices. From searching for information to banking and staying connected with loved ones, these devices hold a treasure trove of personal data.

Protecting this information is crucial to prevent identity theft, fraud, and other cyber threats. By implementing safety measures and good practices, you can safeguard your privacy and that of your family. Here are some tips to reduce your online risks.

Read More

PROTECT YOUR PRIVATE INFORMATION

Be wary of phishing attempts and suspicious links, particularly those requesting personal information. When shopping online, verify the website's security and privacy policies before entering sensitive information. Control what you share on social media to limit exposure of personal information and protect yourself from potential identity theft.

Read More

USE STRONG PROTECTION

Create strong, unique passwords for each online account, consider using a password manager to manage them securely, and explore stronger authentication methods like biometrics or two-factor authentication to enhance security and protect your accounts from unauthorized access.

What is proTECH?

proTECH aims to raise awareness about the dangers of malware, hacking, and phishing in today's digital landscape. Our mission is to educate individuals about these threats and provide them with the knowledge to protect themselves. We strive to empower our audience with the tools and information needed to safeguard their digital lives. Through our efforts, we aim to reduce the incidence of cyber attacks and promote a safer online environment for all. Join us in our mission to secure a better digital future.
